title: "Life gives you what you don't ask for"
---
Life gives you what you don't ask for.
A moral dilemma.
Bad grades.
Few disorders.
Them happy without you.
Misaligned perspective.
A deficit.
Headaches.
An urge.
Curse of self-awareness.
Stomach ache.
Failure.
Suffocation. 

You also get 
A happy, energetic dog.
Books.
Parents.
Few fountain pens.
Music.
Few talents.
Mathematics.
Philosophy. 
A touch.
An embrace. Sometimes.

Trade-offs. Balance. Life.
